CHARACTERS OF BLACK CLOVER

Main Characters

Asta

A determined boy born without magic who wields anti-magic swords. Member of the Black Bulls. Dreams of becoming the Wizard King.

Yuno

Gifted wind magic user and Asta's rival. Member of the Golden Dawn. Calm, focused, and extremely talented.

Noelle Silva

A royal water magic user who initially struggles to control her power but grows into one of the strongest mages in the series.
