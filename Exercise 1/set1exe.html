Garlic Butter Shrimp

Garlic Butter Shrimp is a quick and delicious seafood dish made by cooking fresh shrimp in butter, garlic, and lemon juice. It takes less than 20 minutes to prepare and is perfect for lunch or dinner. The shrimp turns juicy and tender while the garlic butter sauce adds a rich and savory flavor.

Ingredients for Garlic Butter Shrimp

  • 500g Shrimp (Peeled and deveined)
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (optional)
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps on making Garlic Butter Shrimped

  1. Pat the shrimp dry with a paper towel
  2. Season the shrimp with salt, pepper, and paprika (optional)
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat
  4. Add the butter and let it melt completely
  5. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant (do not burn it)
  6. Place the shrimp in the pan in a single layer
  7. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until the shrimp turns pink
  8. Add lemon juice and gently mix everything together
  9. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley
  10. Serve immediately with rice or pasta
