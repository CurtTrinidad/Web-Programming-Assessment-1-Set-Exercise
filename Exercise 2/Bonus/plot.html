PLOT

Black Clover takes place in a world where magic is everything. At the age of fifteen, every person receives a magical grimoire that enhances their natural abilities.

Yuno receives a four-leaf clover grimoire, symbolizing immense talent and good fortune. Asta, who was born without magic, unexpectedly receives a mysterious five-leaf clover grimoire that grants him anti-magic powers—the ability to negate and repel magic.

Both joined the Magic Knights, the kingdom's military force, and begin their journey toward becoming the Wizard King. Along the way, they face powerful threats such as The Eye of the Midnight Sun, devil hosts from the Spade Kingdom, and the Dark Triad. As the story progresses, secrets about the devils, ancient history, and the true nature of magic are revealed.

"My magic is never giving up! – Asta"
